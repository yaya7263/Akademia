Learn Trignometry

Start your journey simple with simple trignometry!

There are three sides to a triangle. For starters, Let's work with a right triangle. We select a Theta( the oval symbol with a line across).

The line opposite theta is called the opposite side, in this case b

The line right next to theta is called the adjacent, in this case a

The line with the longest length is called the hypotenuse

The sin of theta is side opposite over the hypotenuse( divided by)

The cos of theta is side adjacent over the hypotenuse( divided by)

The tan of theta is side opposite over the adjacent( divided by)
